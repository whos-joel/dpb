D Plumbing & Bathrooms is a family owned and operated plumbing business located in Springfield Lakes specialising in maintenance plumbing and bathroom renovations.

Run by a qualified plumber with over twenty years’ experience in the industry, we are proficient in all areas of plumbing, drainage and heating systems from domestic to commercial installations.

Dan Draper qualified as a plumber in 1994 in the UK. He worked in domestic and commercial plumbing specialising in bathroom installations including disability needs and aged care.

Dan arrived in Australia in 2006 and worked as a bathroom designer before going into the construction industry installing plumbing and drainage systems for major projects both government and privately funded including the Queensland Children’s Hospital, the TRI Research Centre at the PA Hospital and many high-rise apartment buildings including the Emporium Hotel.

Dan is a friendly, experienced and reliable tradesman who takes pride in his work. He is licenced and a member of Master Plumbers Association of Queensland.
